Who we are

Insignia Overseas is a pioneer organisation in the field of IELTS & PTE preparation and visa consultation services. We have been empowering young minds to realise their dreams of studying abroad since 2008. Over time, we have established ourselves as one of the most trusted brands in Haryana for offering consultancy services to students and clarifying their doubts. Recognised by the IDP and British Council, our expert guidance and strategies have helped students cross all barriers to achieving education as per global standards. Further, our values and proven track record makes us the most-sought after choice among young students when it comes to career guidance. Come and be a part of our journey that leads to a world full of possibilities.

Our Mission

Founded with the aim of providing excellent solutions for global education, we believe in giving wings to your dreams and transforming your lives. Our mission is to provide international academic and immigrations services to broaden your perspective. Insignia believes in bringing about a revolution in education and making a borderless world. We align our career guidance with that of the global markets helping students achieve their cherished dream of pursuing education in world-class universities and become global citizens.
